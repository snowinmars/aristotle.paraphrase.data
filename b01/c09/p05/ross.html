But according to the necessities of the case and the opinions held about the Forms, if Forms can be shared in there must be Ideas of substances only. For they are not shared in incidentally, but a thing must share in its Form as in something not predicated of a subject (by «being shared in incidentally» I mean that e.g. if a thing shares in «double itself», it shares also in «eternal», but incidentally; for «eternal» happens to be predicable of the «double»). Therefore the Forms will be substance;
